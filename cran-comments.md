## Package positioning
Existing CRAN packages (fairmodels, fairness, fairmetrics) provide
general-purpose fairness metrics and visualization. clinicalfair
focuses specifically on post-hoc fairness auditing of clinical
prediction models, with a lightweight workflow for subgroup metrics,
four-fifths rule screening, threshold-based mitigation, and
intersectional analysis.
